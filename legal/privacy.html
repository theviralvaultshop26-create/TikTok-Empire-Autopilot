Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

TikTok Empire Autopilot ("the App") is a locally-installed automation tool for individual TikTok Shop sellers. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

The App does not operate a server, database, or cloud storage. All data remains on your local device.

2. Data We Access via TikTok API

When you authorize the App through TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow, the App requests the following permissions:

Scope What It Accesses Why It's Needed
user.info.basic TikTok username, display name, avatar URL, follower count To confirm the connected account and display account status in the App
video.upload Ability to upload video files to TikTok To publish product review videos to your TikTok account
video.publish Ability to publish uploaded videos as TikTok posts To complete the video posting workflow (upload + publish)
video.list Read-only list of your posted TikTok videos To display posting history in the App dashboard

3. How Data Is Stored

All data accessed by the App is stored exclusively on your local device:

  • Access tokens and credentials — stored in a local .env file on your machine, never transmitted to any third-party server.
  • Generated content (video scripts, captions, payloads) — saved as plain text files in the local outputs/ folder on your machine.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage data is collected or transmitted by the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party APIs. Their respective privacy policies apply to data sent to those services:

  • TikTok API — TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — used to generate product research and video scripts. Product names and marketing prompts are sent. No personal user data is sent. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Serper.dev — used for Google search queries about trending products. Only search query strings are sent, no personal data. Serper Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share your personal data or TikTok account information with any third parties beyond what is described in Section 4.

6. Token Security

Your TikTok access tokens are stored locally in a .env file on your device. You are responsible for securing access to this file. Tokens expire automatically (access token: 24 hours; refresh token: 365 days) and can be manually revoked at any time via:

  • The App's Settings page → TikTok → Revoke Access
  • TikTok's website → Settings → Security → Manage app permissions

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for business use by adults (18+). It is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
